# User-Journney-Analysis
**This Project is a Follw along project From** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=U-JlXWDqvco

**Final Recommendations** <br>
**1. UX/UI** <br>
- **The Checkout flow**: The conversion rates from Checkout Start to Purchase are excellent (~80%+).The indicates the technical payment flow is frictionless<br>
  - Action: Do not redesign the checkout page rigth now; you risk breaking something that is working perfectly.<br>

**2. Marketing Strategy** <br>
- **Don't Over Investing in Social for Sales**: Social Media is driving 30% of out traffic (Volume) but has ther lowest conversion rate (Efficiency). We are likely to paying for "window shoppers". <br>
  - Action: Shift budget away from "Traffic" objectives on social ads and focus on "Rertargeting" or "Lead Gen" to capture emails instead. <br>

**3. Financial & Revenue**<br>
  - **Audit Ad Spend against AOV**: We found our Average Order Value is ~$115.
    - Action: Set a strict Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) limit. If we are paing more than $30 - $40 to acquire a customer via social media ads (which convert poorly), We are likely to losing money on those specific transactions.
